In the original TransT model, there is only one parameter for class. In our model, instead, we use two parameters to indicate both the class is human face and the human face in template and search region is the same.

We use the Yotube face dataset as the training dataset.

network structure

The training strategy is to split the image into two parts. One is the template 3*128*128, the other is search region 3*256*256 .

We can randomly generate the template and search region pair from the identical person or from different people to form the input.
